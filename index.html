About Me

My name is Abhishek Singh I'm a 26-year-old Front End Engineer based in Gurgaon. I describe myself as a passionate learner, who looks forward to learning from everyone around me. I take great pride in my work, and I always try to better myself with every project I work on. I like to write semantic, cross-browser compatible code by hand that is re-useable, maintainable, and easy to understand.

Aside from my job, I like to create and contribute to open-source projects. That helps me to learn a ton of new stuff, grow as a developer, and support other open-source projects. I enjoy working and helping people to bring a digital presence to their business, personal brand, or portfolio.

In my free time, you can find me in mountains, at historical places or any local events. I love to discover & explore new places, connecting with people which adds to my learning experience. Connect & share is one of the new hobbies that I discover from my travelling nature.

Facts & SkillSet

The main areas of my expertise are React js, Redux HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Angular Js and some WordPress Blogs. Skill level in each language varies. I have designed and develop ERP solutions as well as Full Website Development and Hosting. I can provide personal, Shopify, Dynamic website and Software Solution.

I work as a bridge between graphical design and technical implementation, taking an active role on both sides and defining how the application looks as well as how it works. I work with the UX designer and backend team to build responsive design web applications and manage projects by collaborating with mentors. I also assure that all user input is validated before submitting to back-end. I work as a bridge between the front end and back end code to populate data from back end to the front end for the user- interference. I regularly use version control systems (GIT, SVN) to maintain my code, and I specialize in responsive web design (RWD).
